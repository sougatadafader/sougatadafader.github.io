About Me

Hi,

my name is Sougata Dafader. I am a freelance graphic designer and software developer based in Boston, Massachusetts. My superpower is to create clear and captivating visual content along with optimized backend code. I create applications for Windows, Web and Cell Phones.

After completing my undergraduate degree in Computer Science, I joined a software firm to develop Web/Windows Solutions, Cross Platform applications for Android and IOS. Later I started working for a startup at my hometown. Currently I am pursuing my Masters in Computer Science from Northeastern University, Boston. My capabilities, motivation, tenacity and zeal have been the four corner stones of my career and I believe this strong foundation will help me maintain my steady growth.

My love for coding equals the love for visual designing. I dedicate my career to work closely with my clients and deliver the exact requirement. I have worked with clients of all shapes and sizes in the industry and have found that it is through a team that I achieve my greatest potential. In my leisure time I like to discuss trending ideas, technologies, music, and food recipes. Best part of being a 'Techie' is to meet new people, understanding their requirements,taking up new roles, meeting tight deadlines and trying my best to foresee the possible obstacles. Up till now I have loved facing these challenges and I believe to do so in days ahead. If this sounds exciting, then feel free to contact me for any suitable opportunity.
